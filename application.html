Overview

'Find It' is a sweet and cozy find the object game where you help a young girl find many different objects. You start the day finding toys in her room, then you go with her to to school and meeting her classmates. Then it's time for Halloween and she and her friends are all dressed up! Then it is christmas time and you help build a snowman. Finaly it is the end of the day and the girl reads you a book to sleep.

How to Play

The game is easy to do! All you need to do is point and scroll to find the different objects. Use the scroll to zoom in! If you get stuck don't worry! There is an easy to understand hint system.
